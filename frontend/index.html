About ZeroWaste

ZeroWaste is a community-driven platform that connects people with surplus food to those who need it, reducing food waste and its environmental impact.

Every year, one-third of all food produced is wasted. That's 1.3 billion tons of food, worth nearly $1 trillion. At the same time, millions go hungry. ZeroWaste aims to bridge this gap.
